The Best Movies according to Jessica

My top 5 movies I've watched so far, ranked from 5 (not really good) to 1 (Awesome)

5. Bleach Live-action on Netflix

It was really only the first 2 or 3 episodes squashed together in a movie. 3/10.

4. The Butterfly Effect

A little confusing, but still quite cool. 6/10.

3. Avatar 1

Amazing. The CGI was incredible and the storyline even better. 8.5/10.

2. Avatar: The Way of Water

Loved the story, characters were well thought out and it really tied into the emotional factor. 9/10.

1. Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse

Brilliant movie, the more cartoony feel was amazing and every character was beautifully unique. 10/10.
